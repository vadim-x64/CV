Projects

Guard

An application developed in C# WinForms for Windows that allows users to upload media to check for duplicates. The program uses algorithms to compare photos or videos with each other, identifying exact copies, which allows you to maintain order and avoid unnecessary duplication of files.

Market

A web application developed in Java, that represents dropshipping platform for interaction between sellers and buyers. Clients can register and list their products on the site, but buyers cannot make purchases directly through platform, so they only have opportunity to contact seller to resolve all issues.

Restaurant

The online restaurant is a web service written in JavaScript, where customers can order food for delivery. The project includes a wide selection of dishes, drinks and its own wine list. And the simple and convenient interface allows the user to easily find and order everything he wants. The site is adapted for the phone and is available in any browser.

Spots

The game on C# WinForms is made in the format of a puzzle - spots. The main advantages are an attractive and simple interface, and most importantly - it is a cool game for increasing the level of dexterity, strategy and attention. The game works only under Windows.
